The House Boat —
A Night on the River

Six exclusive floating sanctuaries moored on the Bhagirathi. Where the river becomes your bedroom and the water its music.

House Boat Experience

Six Boats. One River.
Infinite Silence.

Each house boat is a self-contained retreat — comfortable furnishings, private deck, and the unbroken horizon of the Bhagirathi before you. As day fades, the river turns to copper and gold, and the world goes very, very quiet.

Meals are served on the deck. Evenings are yours to spend watching the far shore. Dawn comes with birdsong and river mist — there is nothing quite like it in West Bengal.

River Views

Panoramic Bhagirathi views from every window and private deck.
