Getting Here

Your Journey to Murshidabad

By Train

Take any train to Lalbagh Court Road Station or Berhampore Court, both well-connected from Kolkata (Sealdah/Howrah). From the station, we're just a short 5–20 minute auto ride away.

Kolkata → ~3.5 hrs by Hazarduari Express

By Road

Drive along NH12 from Kolkata — the scenic route passes through Plassey, Krishnanagar, and Berhampore. Several private bus services also run from Kolkata's Esplanade terminal daily.

Kolkata → ~200 km · ~4–5 hrs

By Air

Fly into Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport, Kolkata. From there, take the train or hire a private car to reach Murshidabad. We're happy to help arrange transfers upon request.

Kolkata Airport → ~210 km · Transfers available
