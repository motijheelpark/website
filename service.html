Moti Mahal Restaurant

Where Every Meal Tells
a Murshidabad Story

Moti Mahal is not a dining room — it is an experience. The kitchen honours the culinary traditions of Nawabi Bengal, where spice is an art form.

Moti Mahal

The Heart of the Resort

Our restaurant draws from generations of Bengali culinary heritage — rich Mughal-influenced curries, freshwater fish from the Bhagirathi, aromatic biryanis and slow-cooked kormas that carry the perfume of Murshidabad's spice markets.

Guests often tell us that the meals at Moti Mahal are the part of their stay they miss most. That is our highest honour.

Leisure & Activities

Beyond the Room —
The Full Resort Life

From river evenings to garden strolls — Mahfil-e-Motijheel is a resort that rewards those who wander.

Heritage Tours

Our curated tours take you to Hazarduari Palace, Katra Mosque, Imambara, and the Bhagirathi ghats — with a knowledgeable local guide who brings Murshidabad's 300-year history to vivid life.

House Boat Stays

Six exclusive house boats moored on the Bhagirathi — spend a night rocking gently with the current, waking to the sound of river birds at dawn. An experience unlike anything else in Murshidabad.

Pool & Wellness

Our poolside sanctuary is coming soon — an outdoor pool surrounded by landscaped gardens, a peaceful retreat for morning swims and lazy afternoons.

Garden Walks

The landscaped grounds of Motijheel are a destination in themselves. Morning walks through our gardens, lined with local flora and heritage-inspired pathways, set the tone for each unhurried day.

Photography Excursions

The ghats of Murshidabad, the river at golden hour, the crumbling grandeur of Nawabi ruins — we arrange dedicated photography walks for guests who want to capture the irreplaceable light of Bengal.

Cultural Evenings

On select evenings, Moti Mahal hosts baul music, classical Bengal recitals, or mushaira poetry gatherings — evenings that capture the very spirit that gave this resort its name.
