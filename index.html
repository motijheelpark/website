Our Story

A Gathering Place
Like No Other

Nestled at Motijheel — the Pearl Lake of Murshidabad — our resort sits where the Nawabs of Bengal once walked. Surrounded by three historic municipalities, we are the crossroads of culture, heritage, and warm Bengali hospitality.

Every corner of Mahfil-e-Motijheel whispers history. From the river-facing corridors to the lantern-lit gardens at dusk, this is a place built not just for rest, but for memory.
